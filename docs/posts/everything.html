worlds in my head

26-11-2022

Everything

seeing sees
breathing breathes
feeling feels
thinking thinks

blood flows
heart moans
consciousness grows
ego is no more

clouds fly through the sky
people live people die
people look but don’t see
people run but I’m free

in the void is my head
and my thoughts are in bed
and the life inside me
is finally running free
