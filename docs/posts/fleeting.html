worlds in my head

26-08-2023

Fleeting moments

fleeting moments
on the wind
inside raindrops as they fall
in a cafe through the glass
looking at their stroll

little feelings
drops of time
so easy to walk by
like molasses in summer’s heat
slowly drip around your life

stop and look
they are there
close but feels like far
brief reflections
in the corner of your eye

catch these moments
fleeting puzzles
like a curious child
and from them put together
the story of your life
